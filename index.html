    Who we are

    About AECS4U

    AECS4U Srl is an Italian technology company building an integrated platform for real estate auctions, non-performing loan management, and legal services e-commerce.

    We combine deep domain expertise in Italian property law, cadastral systems, and financial regulation with modern software engineering — from Rust-based AI runtimes to full-stack Python/FastAPI microservices.

    Our repositories reflect a philosophy of building reusable, open-source components wherever possible, while maintaining production-grade quality for our core platform services.

    Real Estate Intelligence

    Property data aggregation, cadastral viewers, auction scraping, and AI-driven valuations

    Legal AI

    Outcome prediction, debt recovery, document OCR, and multi-platform AI assistants

    E-Commerce Platform

    Modular microservices: cart, orders, billing, inventory, search, and notifications

    Scientific Computing

    FEA solvers, sparse linear algebra, structural analysis — from NASA heritage to modern Rust
